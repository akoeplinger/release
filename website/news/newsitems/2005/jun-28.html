Summer of Code Begins

Google's sponsored Open Source Summer of Code has started. Thanks a lot to Chris DiBona and Greg Stein at Google for driving the whole process forward and to Google for funding this effort.

The Mono project will be mentoring sixteen students during the summer, for more information see our Summer 2005 page.

If you were not selected as part of the Summer of Code, you can still join the project and contribute. Let us know if you are insterested.
