New Tutorials, OSX, Third Party Announcements

A couple of new tutorials: Using GConf in your Mono application and Using Notification Icons with Gtk# from Milen Dzhumerov.

Fresh OSX package for Mono 1.1.7 is now available from the downloads page

RemObjects announced its Chrome compiler, an object-oriented Pascal compiler for .NET and Mono. It consumes Gtk# out of the box.

Omnicore announced X-Develop, its cross platform Mono, .NET and Java IDE. It supports Windows, Linux and MacOS X. They offer a 30 day trial of their product. They have a showcase of features on their main page.
