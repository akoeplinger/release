Mono Summit 2007

The Mono Summit 2007 will be hosted by UNED at their Escuelas Pias location venue in Madrid, Spain.

The meeting will be split in two:

  • Mono Contributors: 2-day meeting for contributors to the Mono project on November 26th and 27th.
  • Mono Users and Developers: 3-day meeting for developers and users of the Mono platform.

For more details see our Mono Summit 2007 page.
