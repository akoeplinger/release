Mono at TechEd Barcelona

Novell will be sponsoring a Mono booth at Microsoft's TechEd Developers Conference in Barcelona, Nov 7-10. TechEd is the premier EMEA conference designed to provide developers with highly technical training, information and community resources focusing on building software solutions with Microsoft development tools.

We will also hold an after-hours Mono Discussion in partnership with Mainsoft to provide an overview of the Mono project and roadmap update.
